  # Privacy Policy

  Last updated: May 2026

  ## Your data stays on your device

  Mood Mirror stores all mood entries, tags, and notes **locally on your device only**.
  We do not collect, transmit, or have access to your personal mood data.

  ## What we collect

  **Nothing from mood entries.** Your check-ins, notes, and tags never leave your device.

  **Purchases.** Subscription purchases are handled by Apple App Store or Google Play
  via RevenueCat. RevenueCat may collect anonymous purchase identifiers to validate
  your subscription. See [RevenueCat Privacy Policy](https://www.revenuecat.com/privacy).

  **Crash reports.** If the app crashes, your device's OS may send anonymous crash
  data to us. This contains no mood or personal data.

  ## No account required

  Mood Mirror requires no account, email, or login.

  ## Children

  Mood Mirror is not directed at children under 13.
